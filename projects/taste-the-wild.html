Taste the Wild

Clover Leaf · Art Director · 2025

Clover Leaf Tuna has long been seen as a pantry filler, convenient but not exciting. The challenge was to shift perception from “boring canned food” to something adventurous and desirable. Our strategy was to reconnect the brand to its origin: the untamed ocean. By highlighting the raw power of the sea, we reframed tuna as bold and wild. The line “Taste the Wild” flips the script on canned goods. Paired with dramatic visuals of crashing waves and Clover Leaf branding, the campaign shows consumers that canned food can still feel fresh.
