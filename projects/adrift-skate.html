Adrift Skate

Adrift · Art Director · 2025

More than everyone else, skaters hate being advertised to. So how do we approach that as a small skate shop in Toronto? We use our ad budget to get fined for tagging our ads around the city. It earns us respect and gives us some of the coolest-looking advertising out there. (spec work, not associated with Adrift)
